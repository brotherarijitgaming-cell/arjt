About ARJT

The Mission

ARJT started in 2026 with a simple idea: technology should be useful, not complicated.

My name is Arijit Mondal. I'm a Class 10 student from West Bengal, India. I believe age doesn't define what you can build. This entire website, including all the tools, is coded and designed by me in a single HTML file to prove that point.

ARJT will keep launching micro-tools that help students save time, creators work faster, and anyone get things done without ads, logins, or nonsense.

Why "ARJT"?

It's derived from my name, ARiJiT. But it also stands for: Advanced Resources for Junior Technologists.
